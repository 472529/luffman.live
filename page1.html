Platformer Pt.2

2D platformer

I used C# and the unity engine to create this game. Its a relaxing but challenging demo of a platformer game that I created for a college project. You collect gems, avoid skeletons & jump & climb around 2 levels. The game was mainly designed with mechanics in mind and has a versatile character controller that features wall-jumping and smooth fluid motion.
